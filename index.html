Biography

Dr. Lei Kang is a Beatriu de Pinós PostDoc researcher at Computer Vision Center (CVC), Barcelona, Spain, and serves as a Profesor Asociado Laboral at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB), Barcelona, Spain.

His research interests are document analysis, document visual question answering, document image generation, medical document analysis, and digital humanities.

Education

  • PhD, Computer Vision Center, Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB), Dec. 2020.

  • MSc, Laboratory of Future Networks, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), July 2015.

  • BSc, Automation, Jilin University (JLU), July 2012.
