Biography

Lei Kang received a PhD in Computer Science from Pattern Recognition and Document Analysis Group at Computer Vision Center (Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona) and research group at omni:us in Dec. 2020, which was awarded with an Excellent Cum Laude (highest grade) qualification with Industrial mention.

From May 2021 to June 2023, he was a lecturer at Computer Science Dept. of Shantou University, Shantou, China, and a PI of 3 competitive research projects (National Natural Science Foundation of China, STU Incubation Project for the Research of Digital Humanities and New Liberal Arts, and STU grant).

Currently, he is a PostDoc researcher in the Vision and Language Research Group of Computer Vision Center (CVC), Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB), Barcelona, Spain.

His research interests are document analysis, handwritten text recognition, handwriting generation, human-document interaction and multi-modal multimedia analysis.
